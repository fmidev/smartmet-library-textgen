# Story "weather_overview"

## Story form

The story describes the weather of a 1–N day period in terms of precipitation and cloudiness.

## One-day precipitation

[rain_oneday](rain_oneday.md) describes all possible cases where a single spell of rain both starts and ends on the same day, and the corresponding generated forecasts.

## Two-day precipitation

[rain_twoday](rain_twoday.md) describes all possible cases where a single spell of rain starts on one day and ends on the following day, and the corresponding generated forecasts.
