# Header type "from_until"

Produces a header of the form "Odotettavissa tiistai-illasta torstaiaamuun". Clock times must be 06 or 18, in which case the suffix is morning or evening. Generally used with the corresponding period type "from_until".

Possible headers are:

1. "Odotettavissa maanantaiaamusta maanantaiaamuun" and variations

In Swedish:

1. "Utsikter från måndag morgon till måndag morgon" and variations

In English:

1. "Expected weather from Monday morning until Monday morning" and variations
