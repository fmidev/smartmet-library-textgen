# Header type "clock_range"

The possible headers are:

1. "Odotettavissa kello X-Y"
1. "Odotettavissa maanantaina kello X-Y" and variations

In Swedish:

1. "Utsikter clockan X-Y"
1. "Utsikter på måndag clockan X-Y"

In English:

1. "Expected weather X-Y o'clock"
1. "Expected weather on Monday X-Y o'clock" and variations
