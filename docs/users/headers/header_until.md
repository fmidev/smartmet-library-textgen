# Header type "until"

Produces a header of the form "Odotettavissa tiistai-iltaan asti". The end hour must be 06 or 18, producing a morning or evening specifier respectively. This is generally used with the corresponding period type "until".

Possible headers:

1. "Odotettavissa maanantaiaamuun asti" and variations

In Swedish:

1. "Utsikter till måndag morgon" and variations

In English:

1. "Expected weather until Monday morning" and variations

Phrases used:

* "odotettavissa"
* "asti"
* "N-aamuun", where N=1–7
* "N-iltaan", where N=1–7
