The table can be further simplified to the following generic patterns. The time phrases [Aamulla], [Aamusta alkaen], [Yöllä], etc. must be filled in with combinations consistent with the table above. In some cases the first time phrase may be empty, in which case the sentence refers mainly to the day in question or most of it.

| # | Pattern | English |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | [Aamulla] [sadetta] | [In the morning] [rain] |
| 2 | [Aamulla] [sadetta], [aamulla] [enimmäkseen selkeää] ja poutaa | [In the morning] [rain], [in the morning] [mostly clear] and fair |
| 3 | [Aamulla] [sadetta], [aamulla] poutaa | [In the morning] [rain], [in the morning] fair |
| 4 | [Enimmäkseen selkeää], [aamulla] [sadetta] | [Mostly clear], [in the morning] [rain] |

In case 4 the rain is so short that cloudiness is analysed in practice for the whole day. In case 2 the rain happens at night/before noon so that cloudiness is of interest only for the period after the rain.
